Will your window tint make my house or business super dark inside?

Nope, not unless you want it that way. We have different levels of tint—you can block heat and UV without making your space feel like a cave. Some films are almost clear but still work great for heat and security.

Does the security film really stop someone from breaking in?

The short answer is: it makes it way harder. If someone tries to smash your window, the film holds the glass together so it won’t shatter everywhere. It doesn’t make the window bulletproof or unbreakable, but it buys you time and keeps most break-ins from happening fast, which is what criminals want.

How do I clean my windows after they’re tinted?

Easy—just use a soft cloth and regular glass cleaner (without ammonia). No special products needed. Give the film about a month to fully cure, and after that, you clean them like you normally would.
